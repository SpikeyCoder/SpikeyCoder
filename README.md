# Kevin Armstrong

## tl;dr

Hi there! 👋 I'm Kevin — a builder who's spent the last decade shipping payments and commerce products at Capital One, Walgreens, Amazon, and SpaceX. I started my career as an iOS engineer on the Capital One Wallet team, where I shipped the very first Apple Pay integration on Day 1 using PassKit. From there I moved into product management and spent the next 10 years owning payment method strategy across 13 markets and $116MM+ in GMS — including the launch of [Bizum](https://bizum.es/) for Amazon Prime Spain and the rollout of new local payment methods for Prime members around the world.

I'm currently the founder and lead product engineer at **Armstrong HoldCo LLC** ($112K ARR), where I run a product services and SaaS business focused on PM coaching and SMB tooling. I build the products myself with SwiftUI, AWS, Python, and Supabase, and I bill across two revenue lines (subscriptions + one-off coaching) on a Stripe Connect + Zoho Books ledger.

The common thread across all of these things: I love payments, I love building products that move money safely, and I love that an idea in a notebook can be live on the App Store or a public URL by the weekend.

I'm based in **Chicago, IL**. I have an MBA from the [University of Chicago Booth School of Business](https://www.chicagobooth.edu/) and a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/) ✌️
